I am a User Experience Designer who is passionate about crafting human-centered product experiences. I have been based in Taiwan for the last 8 years. I am fluent in spoken and written Chinese, which has enabled me to work at a high level for two large multinational Taiwan-based organizations. This experience has given me a deep understanding of the Chinese hardware and software development life cycle and Chinese business culture.

I have now moved back to New Zealand and am seeking job opportunities where I can leverage my skills as a human-centered design practitioner to create meaningful products and experiences.
